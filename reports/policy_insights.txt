KEY FINDINGS AND POLICY IMPLICATIONS
============================================================

1. Overall Unemployment Dynamics
   • Average unemployment rate: 11.91%
   • Peak unemployment observed during national lockdown period

2. COVID-19 Impact
   • All areas experienced a 81.4% increase during lockdown
   • Rural areas experienced a 99.9% increase during lockdown
   • Urban areas experienced a 77.8% increase during lockdown

3. Seasonal and Structural Patterns
   • Recurrent monthly fluctuations indicate informal-sector vulnerability
   • Urban unemployment is more volatile and shock-sensitive

4. Policy-Relevant Recommendations
   • Counter-cyclical urban employment programs during crises
   • Expansion of rural employment guarantee schemes
   • Investment in digital and remote-work skills
   • Strengthening labor-market shock absorbers for informal workers

============================================================
